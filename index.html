PROJECT X

Next-Generation Aerodynamics

OUR VISION

To redefine human flight by merging the intuitive grace of a wingsuit with the structural stability of a modern hang glider. Our mission is to democratize high-altitude research through wearable aviation technology.

01. Conceptual Aerofoils

Designed for maximum lift with minimum weight, the wing structure utilizes carbon-fiber ribbing.

02. Integrated Stability

Our unique tail-vane system ensures pitch stability during high-speed descents.

03. Human-Machine Interface

Sensors embedded in the suit's joints translate body movements into control surface adjustments.

The Research Team


Our research project began at the intersection of material science and avian biology. We studied the bone density of predatory birds to develop a skeletal structure for the wing suit that is both lightweight and incredibly resilient to G-forces.

Over the last two years, we have simulated over 5,000 flight hours in virtual environments to ensure the safety and viability of the Daedalus platform.
