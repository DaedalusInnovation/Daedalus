Humans have always dreamed of flight

These are amazing feats of engineering, yet they still fall short of the true, unencumbered flight experience humans desire.

How we can improve:

MORPHING GEOMETRY

By changing shape mid-flight, we optimize aerodynamics for the desired position, mimicking the precision of biological flight.
ULTRA-LIGHTWEIGHT

Our skeletal structures use advanced graphene composites that remain virtually weightless while sustaining extreme air pressure.
ELECTRIC THRUST

High-torque electric turbines provide necessary vertical lift without the bulk of combustion, ensuring a seamless flight profile.
SAFETY NET

An integrated ballistic recovery parachute deploys automatically in milliseconds, providing an absolute failsafe for the pilot.
STABILIZED GLIDE

The active tail design balances the entire suit, providing precise multi-directional movement and efficiency.
INTUITIVE NEURAL-LINK

Translating pilot joint movements into aerofoil control via a high-speed network of distributed bio-sensors.

Putting these together, the FALCON is made.

WINGS

One Wing. Every Function.

Morphing Architecture

We believe that with only one set of wings, a pilot should be able to experience easy takeoff, efficient cruising, fast dives, and quick turns. This versatility is made possible by physically morphing the wings in flight.

Dimensional Properties & Wing Load

To effectively carry a human, we look to nature's most efficient glider: the Wandering Albatross. An albatross typically has a wing load of approx. 24 kg/m². Our engineering goal is to match this natural upper limit.

  • Max Pilot Weight 100 kg
  • Est. Craft Weight 40 kg
  • Total Mass 140 kg
  • Target Wing Load 25 kg/m²
  • Req. Surface Area 5.6 m²

BODY

Safety Core & Propulsion

Ventral Electric Turbine

The primary power source consists of an electric turbine attached to the belly. High-torque output ensures vertical takeoff capability.

TAIL

Stability & Flight Control

Pitch Authority

The Tail is the center of flight controls. It changes the angle of attack to generate lift or initiate precise dives.

SYSTEMS / UI

Augmented Reality Flight Deck

Haptic Feedback

The suit "speaks" to the pilot through localized vibration motors, allowing for instinctive flight reactions without looking at gauges.

SAFETY

Redundancy & Recovery

The Ultimate Failsafe

A Ballistic Recovery System (BRS) deploys a canopy parachute in milliseconds, even in total electrical failure.

STRUCTURE

Materials Science & Integrity

Generative Design

We utilize AI to 3D print titanium nodes connected by carbon fiber, placing material only where structural loads exist.

COST

Accessibility & Economics

Scaling for the Future
