01. Material Research

Our research focuses on the molecular structure of high-tensile carbon weaves. By integrating graphene layers, we have achieved a weight-to-strength ratio that was previously thought impossible in wearable aviation. This section explores the stressors and thermal dynamics of the Daedalus wing ribs during rapid descent.

02. Kinetic Integration

Kinetic integration allows the pilot to become one with the aircraft. The suit's sensors translate elbow and shoulder torque into precise movements of the primary aerofoils. This eliminates the need for heavy mechanical levers, relying instead on intuitive pilot instinct honed through thousand of hours of simulation.

03. Digital Simulation

Every flight begins in the digital twin. Our software suite runs CFD (Computational Fluid Dynamics) analysis on thousands of environmental variables—wind shear, altitude, temperature, and pilot weight—to calculate the safest and most efficient glide paths for the project.
