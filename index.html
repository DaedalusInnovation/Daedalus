DAEDALUS

Aviation Research & Concept Design

OUR VISION

We aim to bridge the gap between high-performance hang gliding and the portability of wingsuit flight. By utilizing advanced carbon-fiber composites and bio-inspired aerodynamics, Daedalus offers a wearable flight platform that demands no compromises on safety or control.

01. Material Research

Utilizing high-tensile graphene weaves to create wings that are lighter than air yet stronger than steel.

02. Kinetic Integration

The suit utilizes the pilot's own movements to adjust control surfaces, creating an intuitive flight experience.

03. Digital Simulation

Extensive wind-tunnel testing and VR simulations ensure every flight path is mapped for maximum efficiency.

The Team

Daedalus began as a specialized research project focused on portable aviation. Our lead researchers come from backgrounds in Aerospace Engineering and Biomechanics.


We believe that human flight should be an extension of the body. Our design process involves studying the skeletal structures of large soaring birds to replicate their efficiency in a wearable format.
